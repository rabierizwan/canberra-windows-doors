## FAQ

**Q: What types of windows do you offer for residential and commercial properties?**
A: Range includes sliding windows, casement windows, awning windows, hung windows, and HT102 windows. Each option serves different functional and design needs.

**Q: Are your products suitable for both new builds and renovations?**
A: Yes. Products can be tailored for new residential and commercial constructions, full-scale renovations and extensions, and targeted upgrades for energy efficiency or design improvements.

**Q: What makes HT102 windows a good choice for Canberra homes?**
A: HT102 windows deliver exceptional thermal and acoustic performance. They're highly energy-efficient, durable, low-maintenance, compatible with double or triple glazing, and suitable for residential and commercial applications.

**Q: Do you assist with product selection and compliance requirements?**
A: Yes. Services include expert guidance for product selection, assistance with building regulations and energy efficiency standards, and tailored configurations for architectural fit.

**Q: What if my order arrives damaged or there is an issue with the product?**
A: Replacements offered for damaged products with prompt support and clear communication to resolve issues efficiently.
